How to make an awesome logo

Assembly time: 30-120 minutes
Awesome rating: 90% awesome

Are you wondering how to design a logo that looks and feels professional? Follow these simple steps to learn how to do just that!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Paper
  • Pen/Pencil (Pencil is preferable because you can easily erase if you make a mistake)
  • Adobe Illustrator (requires subsciption)

  1. Brainstorm ideas for your brand.

    What is this logo for? Personal use? A business? Whatever your logo is for, you need to know the general idea of what you want your logo to say and be before you start. Also, take inspiration from (BUT DO NOT COPY) other successful logos when designing your own.

  2. Choose the one you like the best and draw a rough sketch of it.

    This will help you to edit the logo quickly and easily, and to get any additional ideas you may have onto paper.

    Logo sketches from user josh question mark on Flickr.

  3. Transfer your logo into Adobe Illustrator.

    Take a clear photo of your sketch, import the photo, and lower its opacity. This will allow you to start designing a computer file of your logo.

  4. Design your logo and add a border to it.

    Edit your logo as you design it. Don't be rigid in your design, let your creativity flow. Make sure to add a border to your logo to make it both look professional and make it easier to use.

  5. Save and export the file for further use.

    Congratulations! You have officially designed your very own logo!.
